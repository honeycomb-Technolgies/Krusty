Privacy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Your data stays local

Krusty runs entirely on your machine. Sessions, credentials, and workspace files are stored in ~/.krusty and never leave your device unless you explicitly send them to an AI provider via API calls.

AI provider communication

When you use Krusty, your prompts and tool outputs are sent to the AI provider you configured (MiniMax, OpenAI, Z.AI, or OpenRouter). Each provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle that data. Krusty does not proxy, log, or retain any data in transit.

This website

This marketing site is static HTML. It does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. No personal data is collected.

No telemetry

Krusty does not phone home, collect usage metrics, or send crash reports. The binary makes no network requests except those you initiate (AI provider calls, MCP servers, Tailscale).
